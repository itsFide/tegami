All knives belong to the Japanese brand Damascus, which translates to 'Letter.' This name symbolizes delicate, precise work: preparing culinary ingredients with exceptional care, like a letter written by hand in calligraphy.

Each knife is made of 67 layers of Damascus steel.
The central layer is premium VG-10 steel, which retains its sharpness perfectly but is less impact-resistant.
To protect it, 33 layers of 4116 steel are added on each side. This steel is highly durable and easy to sharpen. Thanks to a special forging technique, it creates a beautiful pattern on the blade with shimmering effects. As a result of this combination, the knife maintains its sharpness for over 6 months and is resistant to damage and corrosion.
