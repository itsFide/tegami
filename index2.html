What are Tegami knives, and what makes them unique?

Tegami is a premium brand of Japanese kitchen knives made from Damascus steel, designed for those who value durability, versatility, reliability, sharpness, and style.

    Key features of these knives:

  • Razor-sharp blade thanks to VG-10 steel in the center and 66 layers of 4116 steel on the sides.
  • Unique pattern on the blade surface: no other knife has this. It also reduces sticking.
  • Ergonomic handle made from maple burl and epoxy resin with anti-slip protection.
  • Sealed construction for easy cleaning and protection against bacteria, meeting Japanese JIS standards.

You might have these questions

How often do you need to sharpen Tegami knives?

Each knife retains its sharpness for about six months. After that, simply touch it up with a sharpening stone, sharpener, or honing rod.

What type of steel is used in the knives, and how many layers do they have?

Each knife is made of 67 layers of combined Damascus steel, consisting of 4116 and VG-10 grades.

How quickly is the knife delivered?

Delivery takes anywhere from a few days to a few weeks, depending on your location.

Is the delivery paid or free?

Delivery is free for orders of three or more knives. For fewer knives, the delivery cost is $9.95.

Is this an original product?

Yes, this is an original product, directly from the workshop of blacksmiths with over 20 years of experience.

And what if I don't like it?

You can return the product within 30 days, no questions asked.
